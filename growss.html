Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

: 1 cups sugaro four

  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Step

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix all the dry ingredients.
  3. Add milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  4. pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
